Chicken Katsu

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Panko
  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chili flakes
  • Flour

Preperation

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper and chili flakes
  2. Beat eggs and place in container big enough to place the chicken breast in it
  3. Cover seasoned chicken with flour
  4. Coat the chicken in eggwash
  5. Coat the chicken in panko
  6. Fry on medium high heat until golden
