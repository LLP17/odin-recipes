Fettucine Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Fettucine Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesean
  • Butter
  • Grated garlic
  • Cilantro

Preperation

  1. Boil hot water with oil and salt to taste
  2. Add Fettucine to boiled water
  3. Melt butter and season with grated garlic
  4. Simmer heavy cream with the melted grated garlic
  5. Add heavy cream mixture to cooked fettucine pasta
